Beet Salsa

Tired of plain ol' Carne Asada Tacos? Get exotic with beet salsa.

  • 4 - 6 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 - 2 Serrano pepper
  • Reasonably sized onion (green or vidalia)
  • 10 garlic cloves (leave it up to your buds, I like 10 or more)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 bunch of beets

1 or more lemons
