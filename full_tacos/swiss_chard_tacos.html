Swiss Chard Tacos

Got chard or another leafy green from your CSA? Now you can have tacos.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Chard
  • 1 Onion sliced thin
  • 1/2 Cup of chicken broth
  • Crumbled queso fresco or crema
  • Salsa

Of course, you could decide you want to add some grilled chicken or steak. Or you could make it Monteiro and add tofu or other soy products.
